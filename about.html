VR Creative Directors

Big rigs on A Beach

driving in soft resistance,

The fumes are for me;

How can such dirty water

Be so gentle?

I feel like the most important thing I can offer you is a story; different hues on kaleidoscopic wheels transversing from the collective logos into the individual huemans who will, without a shadow of doubt, try to express with light and sound what they are in awe of. I understand its a very one sided conversation that takes place the "I" tells the "You". However the ideas and feelings that come post-experience and how those spring forth a true conversation is what I am utterly devoted too. It's such a magical feeling and if I can thicken the pot then by god, that's exactly what I'm going to do.
